## Communication Channels

We use a variety of communication tools to ensure clear and effective collaboration within the team and with external stakeholders.

### Internal Channels
- **Slack**: For quick communication and team-wide announcements.
- **Email**: For formal communication and detailed discussions.
- **Confluence**: For documenting processes, decisions, and collaborative efforts.

### External Channels
- **Email**: For communicating with external stakeholders and partners.
- **Zoom**: For virtual meetings with external teams and collaborators.
- **Project Management Tools**: For tracking tasks and coordinating projects with external teams.
