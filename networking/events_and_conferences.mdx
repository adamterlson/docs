## Events & Conferences

We participate in a variety of industry events and conferences to stay updated on the latest trends and share our knowledge with the broader community.

### Key Events
- **Annual Tech Summit**: A gathering of industry leaders to discuss the latest advancements in technology.
- **Open-Source Conference**: A forum to present our open-source contributions and learn from others in the community.
- **Cloud Innovation Expo**: A conference dedicated to the latest trends in cloud computing and infrastructure.

### Participation
- **Speaking Engagements**: Team members often present on topics such as cloud architecture, security, and DevOps best practices.
- **Networking Opportunities**: These events provide a platform for us to network with other professionals, vendors, and potential collaborators.
