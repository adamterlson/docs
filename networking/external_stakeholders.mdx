## External Stakeholders

We maintain active communication with several key external stakeholders who play a vital role in our team's success.

### Key Stakeholders
- **Clients**: Direct beneficiaries of our work; we maintain regular updates and feedback sessions with them.
- **Sponsors**: Individuals or groups who provide resources and support for our initiatives.
- **Partners**: External organizations that collaborate with us to achieve shared objectives.

### Engagement Methods
- **Monthly Check-ins**: Scheduled meetings to update on progress and gather feedback.
- **Quarterly Reviews**: In-depth discussions on performance and strategic alignment.
- **Feedback Surveys**: Periodic surveys to understand stakeholder satisfaction and areas for improvement.
