## Diversity & Inclusion

We are committed to building a team that embraces diversity and fosters inclusion, where every voice is heard and valued.

### Why It Matters
Diversity brings a wide range of perspectives, experiences, and ideas, enabling us to create more innovative and inclusive solutions.

### Our Approach
- **Hiring Practices**: We aim to attract a diverse pool of talent through inclusive hiring practices.
- **Inclusive Culture**: We encourage open communication and celebrate the uniqueness of every team member.
- **Ongoing Support**: We provide resources and support to ensure everyone feels welcome and empowered to contribute.

### Initiatives
- **Diversity Training**: Regular workshops to raise awareness and promote inclusivity within the team.
- **Employee Resource Groups**: Communities within the organization to support underrepresented groups.
