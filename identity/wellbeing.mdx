## Team Wellbeing

We prioritize the well-being of our team members, recognizing that a healthy, happy team is key to sustained success.

### Mental Health
We provide resources and support for mental well-being, encouraging team members to take care of themselves and each other.

### Work-Life Balance
We promote a balanced approach to work, ensuring that team members have the time and flexibility to manage personal and professional responsibilities.

### Physical Health
We support initiatives that encourage physical activity, healthy living, and overall well-being.

### Team Support
We foster a culture where open communication about challenges is encouraged, and resources are available for anyone needing help or support.
