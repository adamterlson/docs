## North Star Statement

Provide a robust, secure, and scalable cloud infrastructure that enables seamless development, deployment, and operations at SiriusXM.

## Our Mission

To build and maintain a solid cloud foundation that supports efficient, reliable, and secure application development and deployment across the organization.

## Our Vision

To establish a world-class cloud infrastructure that ensures high availability, performance, and security, empowering development teams to innovate and deliver value rapidly.
