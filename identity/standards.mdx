## Engineering Standards

We follow a set of high standards to ensure our work is consistent, reliable, and of the highest quality.

### 1. Code Quality
We maintain clean, readable, and well-documented code that adheres to best practices.

### 2. Testing & Validation
All code must be thoroughly tested and validated before deployment, ensuring reliability and stability.

### 3. Security
Security is embedded in every step of our development process to protect our systems and data.

### 4. Performance
Our solutions are optimized for performance to ensure scalability and efficiency.

### 5. Continuous Improvement
We regularly review and improve our processes to adapt to new challenges and opportunities.
